SERVICES

Check out our trips

horseriding

Join us for a two-hour horseback ride along the beach and through the desert. Snap photos and videos as you ride alongside the sea, feeling the rush of wind and waves. Take a dip with your horse in the sea, then relax with an optional lunch. It's a day of adventure and beauty you won't forget!

Quad tour

Join our exciting Quad tour safari for 3 or 5 hours! Start at the safari station garage with a welcome drink, then ride 25 km on a Quad to a Bedouin village. Enjoy Bedouin tea, a camel tour, and panoramic views perfect for pictures. Explore the Bazar and pharmacy, then ride back 25 km to the station. Finish the adventure with a fun party and an open buffet dinner.

super safari

Join our exciting super safari tour, Start at the safari station garage with a welcome drink, then ride 20 minutes on a Quad then ride spider cars for 20 minutes then take the jeep cars to a Bedouin village. Enjoy Bedouin tea, a camel tour, and panoramic views perfect for pictures. Explore the Bazar and pharmacy, then ride back with the jeep to the station. Finish the adventure with a fun party and an open buffet dinner.

dolphins trip

Embark on an unforgettable dolphin trip! Enjoy free soft drinks on the boat as we search for dolphins and capture videos of these amazing creatures. We'll make two snorkeling stops to explore vibrant marine life. A delicious lunch will be served on the boat. Join us for a day of adventure and fun on the water!

paradise island

Join us for an unforgettable trip to Paradise Island! Enjoy free soft drinks on the boat as we head to the island, where you'll spend 2 hours exploring and relaxing. We'll make two snorkeling stops to discover vibrant marine life. A delicious lunch will be served on the boat. Come along for a day of adventure and fun on the water!
